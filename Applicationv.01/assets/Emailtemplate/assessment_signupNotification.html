Student Instructions – Before your Start:

  1. Use the link provided to Sign Up for the Assessment Centre & Read the instructions carefully including the Technical Requirements.

Student Instructions: On the Assessment Day

  1. Ensure you are well set up and meet all the Technical Requirements.

  2. It may be a good idea to do a Mock Assessment if you are using the Assessment for the first time. It would set you up well for the Assessments.

  3. Ensure you have received the Student Passkey on your registered email id at least 24 hours before the scheduled assessment.

  4. In case you do not receive the Passkey please contact your assigned proctor, the contact details of the proctor are available when you click on the view more link of your scheduled assessment in the Assessment Center.

  5. Keep your Identification (ID) document, the same ID that you uploaded while completing your student details ready for verification by the proctor.

  6. Log in the Assessment Centre and the Join the Virtual Meeting at the specified time before the Scheduled Assessment time as communicated.
